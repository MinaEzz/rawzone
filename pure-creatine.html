Product Overview

Description
L-Arginine is a critical amino acid that plays a vital role in the synthesis of nitric oxide, an essential compound required for blood circulation. Studies have demonstrated that L-Arginine-derived nitric oxide can provide significant benefits for wound healing and overall circulation, as well as enhancing mental clarity, immune function, and memory. Furthermore, research suggests that L-Arginine supplementation can increase muscle mass and improve endurance, as it is a precursor to creatine, which helps supply energy to all cells of the body.
In summary, L-Arginine is an excellent supplement for individuals looking to improve their athletic performance, support their immune system, and enhance their overall physical well-being.
Helps support immune system
Helps support athletic endurance
Helps support recovery from athletic performance
Suggested Use
Take 3-5 grams half an hour before exercise and on rest days. You can take the dose throughout the day meals to provide the body with nutrients and better muscle recovery.
