product Overview

Beta Alanine acts as a precursor to the production of nitric oxide (NO) and works to reduce acidification within the muscles resulting from intense exercise and intense training. Beta Alanine delays muscle fatigue allowing you to increase your performance. and it is an indispensable ingredient for your workout regimen as it serves as a precursor to nitric oxide production, which significantly reduces muscle acidification associated with intense exercise. By delaying muscle fatigue, it enables you to achieve peak performance and surpass your previous limits. Don't wait to add beta-alanine to your routine and unlock your full potential.

Suggested Use

Take 3-5 grams half an hour before exercise and on rest days. You can take the dose throughout the day meals to provide the body with nutrients and better muscle recovery.
